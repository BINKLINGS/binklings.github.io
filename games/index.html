New Vision

With Unreal Engine 5, binklings' games can achieve 8K quality, super dynamic lighting and reflection. You will be able to experience the best details in the super-large world and feel the charm of the next generation of games.


New excitement

Realistic scenes and aural effects will put you in the scene and make you feel the new sense of excitement and substitution.


New Degree of freedom

In this infinite world, you can change everything you see and write your own history. With the Unreal Engine and many plugins, as well as a self-developed game system, BINKLINGS 'games have taken it's game' freedom to a new level.


New Degree of truth

In addition to realistic images and degrees of freedom, BINKLINGS GAMES also uses the world's leading physics engine technology for simulation. Whether it is conventional physics or chaos simulation, BINKLINGS GAMES will bring you new experiences.

Internal Test Plan

The game is currently in beta 0.6.5 and is expected to be released in 2023 as a pre-release.
